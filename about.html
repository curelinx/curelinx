About CURELINX

CURELINX is an emerging start-up specializing in the trading of disposable medical devices, dedicated to serving the Gulf region. Our mission is to bridge the gap between high-quality medical suppliers and healthcare providers by ensuring efficient, reliable, and cost-effective distribution of essential medical products.

  • Source high-quality disposable medical devices from trusted manufacturers & distributors in India.
  • Supply bulk medical products to hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities in the Gulf.
  • Ensure seamless logistics, compliance, and timely delivery to support healthcare excellence.
